How to Use in AEM

  1. Deploy the ClientLib files to your AEM instance
  2. Add the "Wheel Spin Game" component to your page
  3. Configure the prizes, colors, and redeem codes using the AEM dialog
  4. The component will automatically initialize with your settings
  5. Users can spin the wheel and receive prizes with redeem codes
